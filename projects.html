Icebot

it's alive!

Started in early May of 2017, Icebot is a discord bot for Paragon-related Discord channels. Designed for Paragon, it contains a queue system, for people looking for groups to play with. There is also a deck system (which is no longer up-to-date with Paragon) that allows people to save their own decks and get other people's.

LampPost

you lean against it and listen to music

Created January 2017, the LampPost is my only program with a GUI. It can play music (that repeats), with adjustable volume as well as an email sender which sends emails through Gmail.
(Email does not work unless you have less secure apps turned on in google settings).

Bitwise Tic Tac Toe

now with only 32 bits

Using bitwise operators, all the data in the Tic Tac Toe game is within just two 16 bit integers! The game is best played with a friend (local multiplayer) and to play it you need to launch it via cmd or the terminal for your OS.
